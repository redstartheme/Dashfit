Roles

Versatile Role Management for Your Needs

Luxuria is a comprehensive template designed to streamline role management across multiple user types. With this single template, you can effectively manage different roles such as Admin & Employee, and you have the flexibility to create additional roles tailored to your specific requirements. Here are some of the standout features:

  • keyboard_arrow_right Unified Template for All Modules: Seamlessly manage both Admin and Employee functionalities within a single template.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightCustom Role Creation: Easily add multiple roles to accommodate your organization's unique structure and requirements.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightRole-Based Authentication: Enhance security with authentication processes tailored to user roles.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightAccess Control: Restrict page access based on user roles, ensuring that sensitive information is only available to authorized users.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightDynamic Sidebar Menu: Enjoy a sidebar menu that adapts based on the users role, providing a personalized navigation experience.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightIntegrated AuthGuard Pages: Protect critical pages with integrated AuthGuard, ensuring that only authenticated users can access them.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightDedicated Modules: Benefit from separate modules for Admin & Employee, facilitating specialized functionality for each role.

Admin

The Admin role is the backbone of the Luxuria template, empowering you to oversee and control all modules effectively. With extensive capabilities, the Admin can manage key areas such as occupancy, bookings, housekeeping, payments, rooms, staff, and more. Here’s a closer look at the features available:

  • keyboard_arrow_rightDashboard Overview: Monitor and manage all hotel data through an intuitive dashboard, offering real-time insights into operations and performance.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightBooking Management: Efficiently handle room reservations, cancellations, and modifications to optimize occupancy rates.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightRooms Management: Oversee room availability, assignments, and maintenance to ensure a seamless guest experience.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightStaff Management: Manage staff schedules, roles, and performance, enhancing operational efficiency and service quality.
  • keyboard_arrow_right Departments Management: Coordinate various hotel departments, such as housekeeping, front desk, and maintenance, for streamlined operations.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightHousekeeping Management: Track cleaning schedules and room status to maintain high standards of cleanliness and guest satisfaction.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightLeave Management: Monitor and manage employee leave requests to ensure adequate staffing at all times.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightCabs Management: Arrange transportation services for guests, ensuring convenience and timely pickups.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightPayment and Billing Management: Handle all financial transactions, including guest payments and billing, for accurate revenue tracking.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightReports Management: Generate detailed reports on occupancy, revenue, and staff performance to inform strategic decisions.

Employee

The Employee role in the Luxuria template is designed to streamline daily operations for hotel staff, providing them with essential tools to manage their tasks effectively. Here’s an overview of the key features available to hotel employees:

  • keyboard_arrow_rightPersonal Dashboard: Access a customized dashboard that provides an overview of essential information and tasks, helping employees stay organized and informed.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightAttendance Management: Check and manage attendance records easily, ensuring accurate tracking of working hours.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightLeave Module: Submit leave requests and monitor leave balances, allowing for better planning and management of time off.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightPayroll Management: Access payroll information, including salary details and bank information, ensuring transparency and ease of financial management.
  • keyboard_arrow_rightShift Management: View shift details, including schedules and assignments, to stay updated on work commitments.
